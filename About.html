Biography

Guillaume Tomasi (b.1984) is an emerging photographer who was born in France and currently lives in Montreal. His work was featured in various publications (both printed and online) and collectives expositions in Montreal, London and Bienne (Switzerland). He is currently completing his BFA in Photography at Concordia University and he created in 2016 the collaborative project Fiiiirst which features anonymous image based discussions between authors photographers. His photographic practice is mainly based on exploration, wandering and luck. Recently, his work moved into an obsessed research about our world perception based on personal events.

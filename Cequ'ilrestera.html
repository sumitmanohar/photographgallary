Ce qu'il restera
2015-2016



All things come to an end.


Each species on Earth can disappear at any time, yet we still have difficulty to imagine that we can also become an endangered
species. Our pace of life is still causing irreversible modifications on the environment, and is certainly the cause of our
uncertain fate. "Ce qu'il restera" proposes to observe the alterations of the man on his habitat, and to record what would
be the next fossils which will testify of our presence on Earth if we disappear.
